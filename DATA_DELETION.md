# Account & Data Deletion Instructions

**Last Updated:** 11/17/2025

At **NovaED**, we value your privacy and provide you with full control over your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and permanently remove your information from our systems, please follow one of the methods outlined below.

---

## Option 1: In-App Deletion (Instant)

You can delete your account directly through the NovaED application settings. This is the fastest method.

1.  Open the **NovaED** app.
2.  **Log in** using the account you wish to delete.
3.  Navigate to the **Profile** section.
4.  **Scroll down** to the bottom of the screen.
5.  Click on the button labeled **"Remove my account"**.
6.  Confirm your choice if prompted.

*Your account will be immediately deactivated and queued for deletion.*

## Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the application or prefer to request deletion manually, you may contact the administrator directly.

*   **Email:** [ahmedbkry204@gmail.com](mailto:ahmedbkry204@gmail.com)
*   **Subject Line:** Please use **"Account Deletion Request"** as the subject.
*   **Requirements:** Please send the email from the address associated with your NovaED account, or explicitly state the email address of the account you wish to delete.

*Manual requests are typically processed within 2-3 business days.*

---

## Consequences of Deletion

In accordance with our [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md), once your account is deleted:

*   **Personal Identity:** Your name, email address, and Google ID are permanently erased from our database.
*   **App Data:** All quiz attempts, student answers, and performance results associated with your account are deleted.
*   **Irreversibility:** This action **cannot be undone**. If you wish to use NovaED again in the future, you will need to create a new account.
