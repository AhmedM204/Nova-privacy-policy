# NovaED – Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 11/17/2025

NovaED (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a learning and assessment platform designed to help students prepare for exams. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application and services.

By using NovaED, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1. Personal Information

We collect the following personal data when you create an account:

*   Full name
*   Email address
*   Google Account information (for Google Sign-In authentication)

Creating an account is required to use the application.

### 1.2. Usage & App Activity Data

We collect data related to how users interact with the app, including:

*   Student answers and quiz attempts
*   Performance results
*   App navigation and behavior analytics

This data is used to improve the user experience and enhance platform features.

### 1.3. Analytics & Tracking

We use Firebase services (such as Firebase Analytics) to collect:

*   Device information
*   App usage statistics
*   Crash reports
*   Behavioral analytics

Firebase may collect certain identifiers for analysis purposes.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

*   User authentication
*   Account management
*   Tracking progress and storing quiz results
*   Improving app performance and user experience
*   Service optimization and analytics
*   Ensuring platform security and preventing abuse

We do not show advertisements.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third-party companies for advertising or marketing.

We may share anonymized analytics data with service providers such as Firebase for operational and diagnostic purposes.

No personally identifiable information is shared with any external party except where required by law.

## 4. Children and Underage Users

NovaED may be used by individuals under 18. We take privacy very seriously and ensure data is handled securely according to international standards.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

### 5.1. Personal Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as the user has an active account.

### 5.2. User Data Deletion

Users have the right to request deletion of their account and all associated personal data. Upon deletion:

*   The user's name, email, Google ID, and all identifiable information are permanently erased.
*   Quiz answers, results, logs, and any identifiable records are deleted.
*   Only anonymized, non-identifiable aggregated analytics remain stored for statistical and improvement purposes.

Anonymized data cannot be linked back to any individual user.

## 6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect stored data, including:

*   Encrypted communication between the app and backend
*   Secure storage of user information
*   Access control for sensitive data

Despite our efforts, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Third-Party Services

NovaED uses Google Sign-In and Firebase. These services may collect device and usage data according to their respective policies.

You may review their privacy policies here:

*   **Google Privacy Policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
*   **Firebase Privacy & Security:** [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## 8. Future Features

If future updates introduce new permissions (such as notifications), users will be informed and may be asked for additional consent.
